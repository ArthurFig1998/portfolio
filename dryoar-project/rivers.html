Check Out Our Rivers!

Jackson Hole

Jackson Hole Whitewater and it’s entire crew are looking forward to meeting you this summer. We offer Whitewater and Scenic Float Trips beginning the first of May through October. Voted the #1 rafting company in Jackson Hole.

We are located in the town of Jackson Hole, about 30 minutes from Grand Teton National Park and about an hours drive from Yellowstone National Park. You can book online or if you would like to talk to one of our professional staff please give us a call. See you on the river!

Yellowstone River

Summer is just around the corner and before you know it we will be enjoying the warmth and beauty of summer in Yellowstone country!

The dark days of winter have us dreaming of rollercoaster waves, splashes spraying everyone in the raft, and calm stretches filled with laughter and good conversation. Now is the perfect time to lock in your seats on one of our outstanding trips and we hope you'll join us this summer with your friends and family on the mighty and beautiful Yellowstone River!
